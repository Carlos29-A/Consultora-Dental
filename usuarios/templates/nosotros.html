Nosotros

Our office recommends that patients schedule regular check-ups at intervals recommended by one of our dentists. Check-ups allow our dentists to identify early signs of dental problems before they can cause irreversible damage to your teeth and gums.

During your check-up, your dentist will perform a visual inspection of your teeth and gums using specialized dental instruments. Radiographs, including dental x-rays, may be required. If your visit includes impacted wisdom teeth, periodontitis, loose fillings, or other dental problems, these can be treated during your check-up or may require a follow-up visit.
